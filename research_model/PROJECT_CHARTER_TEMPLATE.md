## Purpose

This document defines the scope, goals, constraints, and success criteria
for the [Project Name] research project.

It is the stable anchor for all research work. It does not contain findings
— those live in research artifacts. It describes why the research exists,
what questions it must answer, and what it explicitly does not investigate.
